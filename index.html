Screwdriver Belt Holder

After ordering a Screwdriver from Linus Tech Tips, I realized that I wanted a way to carry the screwdriver in a better way than simply holding it in my back pocket, as such I designed a holster that could clip to my belt and safely store the screwdriver while I'm not using it. The holster is designed to be 3D printed, with support only on the clip portion, a TPU sleeve is printed seperately, and is designed to fit in the hard plastic holster with an interference fit below, you should see a 3D model of the completed project compiled in Solidworks Visualize and displayed here by use of the model-viewer package.
